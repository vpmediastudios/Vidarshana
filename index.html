About Me

Who I Am

I'm Vidarshana Pramuditha — most people know me as Vidu. I'm a graphic designer and creative entrepreneur from Sri Lanka.

Design has always been my language. I see the world in visuals — colors, type, composition, movement. As a graphic designer, I craft brand identities, visual systems, and digital experiences that communicate with clarity and impact.

Beyond design, I'm the proud founder of The Village Kitchen Hiriketiya — a place where the flavors of Sri Lanka's south coast come alive, run by our incredible family. It's more than a restaurant; it's a feeling, a gathering spot, a slice of coastal life.

I also founded VP Media Studios — a graphic design studio and internet missions management studio where creativity meets strategy. We don't just design; we engineer digital presence.

In addition, I'm actively engaged with Thevindu Nethum's Songs — my childhood friend. We share a really close friendship, and I help with his content, bringing creative direction, visual branding, and strategic support to amplify the music.
